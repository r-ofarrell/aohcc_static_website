Anchors of Hope Counseling Center

Located in the West Ashley neighborhood of beautiful Charleston, SC, we are a group of therapists who have come together with a shared vision for providing high quality and evidence-based mental health services to families, children, adolescents, and adults. Our experienced therapists bring their clinical expertise, forged through years of training and counseling experience, and combine it with a passion for supporting the mental health needs and flourishing of all who come through our doors.
