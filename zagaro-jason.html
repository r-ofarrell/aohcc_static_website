Jason Zagaro, LPC

Jason Zagaro is a Licensed Professional Counselor who works via telehealth with clients across the state of South Carolina. Jason is an accomplished yoga instructor, having owned and operated his own yoga studio for years prior to entering into the mental health field. His passion for mental health stems from his own personal experiences, which have instilled in him a deep commitment for supporting others and facilitating transformative and healing growth.

His approach to therapy is highly individualized and he collaboratively works to establish clear goals and expectations with each client he meets with. His approach is rooted in Cognitive Behahvioral, Psychodynamic, Mindfulness-based, and Positive psychology approaches. He works alongside clients to help them gain deeper insights and to build coping skills that will help them to effectively navigate their fears and life pressures.

Jason specializes in working with teens, young adults, and adults who are struggling with anxiety, depression, ADHD, OCD, trauma-related difficulties, relationship difficulties, stress management, and addiction. Jason also has experience providing couples therapy that enables couples to navigate current relationship difficulties. In addition to the above, Jason is well-versed in current practices related to nutrition, exercise, and weight loss and has a passion for assisting clients in their pursuit of holistic health and well-being.

Jason bring a calm, accommodating, and personable demeanor to the therapy room. He prioritizes creating a safe and open environment in therapy where client feel comfortable sharing honestly and where he can provide honest and supportive feedback.
